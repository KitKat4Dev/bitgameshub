How to Create Your Story

  1. Enter your story idea in the prompt box
  2. Choose the type of creepypasta that best fits your story
  3. Click "Generate Random Prompt" to get an AI-generated prompt based on your selected type
  4. Select a narrative style to determine how your story will be told
  5. Add optional secondary and third narrative styles for more complexity
  6. Choose a sub-style to give your story a specific horror focus
  7. Set word count and time of day for your story
  8. Select weapons if they're part of your narrative
  9. Choose an ending type to determine how your story concludes
  10. Pick emoji level and text style to set the tone
  11. Click "Generate Creepypasta" to create your story
